## Implementation Notes

- Treat ranged weapon speed like melee speed.
- Beyond max range band: impossible to hit.
- With a ranged weapon drawn, combatants can walk backwards at 5 ft per second.
- Thrown weapons use STR only when running toward an enemy at 10 ft/s or faster (ignore for now).
- Stationary targets defend with d12p; moving targets defend with d20p. No other modifiers except shield bonus.
