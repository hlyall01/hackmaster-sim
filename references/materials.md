# HackMaster 5e — Materials and Tier Effects

## General Effects by Tier
- Melee and thrown weapons: Increased Attack and Damage per tier.
- Ranged weapons: Increased Damage per tier; require +1 Strength per tier.
- Projectiles: Increased Attack and Damage per tier (stacks with the ranged weapon’s tier).
- Armor: Increased Damage Reduction per tier; reduced Defense penalty (cannot go above +0).
- Shields: Increased Damage Reduction and Defense per tier.
