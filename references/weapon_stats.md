## Notes

- *speed is 5 if opponent is unarmed or unaware  
- D indicates weapon may be used with two one-handed weapon fighting styles  
- H indicates weapon specifically designed for saddle-borne combat (see Mounted Combat on p. 233)  
- T indicates two-handed weapon  
- C can only move at a walking speed while actively crouching behind a shield  

**Legend:**
- `[p]` = penetrating die  
- `^1` = ignores 1 DR vs. 5+ Dr
- `^2` = ignores 2 DR vs. 5+ Dr
- `^3` = applies +1 DR vs. 5+ Dr

A damage type of **E** indicates a weapon capable of ensnaring. Being hit by an ensnaring weapon prevents you from many things, including moving and performing special combat moves. To break free of an ensnaring weapon, you must succeed on either a Feat of Strength or Escape Artist check, or destroy the ensnaring weapon by attacking it directly.
